Heating, Ventilation & Air Conditioning (HVAC)

Ventilation and heating services are critical to user comfort, productivity, and health in any commercial, industrial, or residential development. By partnering with Nexus Link, clients secure complete peace of mind knowing that their HVAC configurations are running smoothly, safely, and with maximal energy efficiency.

  • Intricate chiller plants and VAV systems
  • High-efficiency particulate air (HEPA) filtration units
  • Smart thermostat and BMS integration
  • Engineered for extreme Middle East ambient temperatures
  • Energy-optimized designs to reduce operational costs

Industrial Cooling & Vertical Transportation

Our technical teams flawlessly integrate heavy-duty chillers, district cooling networks, and sophisticated elevator/escalator systems. We understand that vertical transportation is the vital circulatory system of any high-rise.

  • Heavy-duty district cooling network integration
  • High-capacity industrial chiller installation
  • Elevator & escalator system commissioning
  • Peak-hour traffic optimization design
  • Minimal maintenance downtime engineering

High, Medium & Low Voltage Distribution

Electricity is the lifeblood of modern facilities. We engineer, supply, and install comprehensive end-to-end power distribution networks. From coordinating main high-voltage substations and transformers to configuring localized low-voltage consumer panels, we ensure absolute stability.

  • Main HV substation and transformer coordination
  • Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS) systems
  • Heavy-duty diesel generator backup systems
  • Automatic Transfer Switches (ATS)
  • Mission-critical redundancy design
🤖

Intelligent Automation & Specialized Lighting

Nexus Link deploys intelligent industrial automation and smart lighting control systems that adapt to occupancy and natural daylight. Our electrical engineers execute stringent setups for fundamental life safety.

  • Occupancy-adaptive smart lighting systems
  • Addressable fire alarms and smoke control panels
  • Public address systems for emergency evacuation
  • Centralized CCTV and Access Control networks
  • Building Management System (BMS) integration
